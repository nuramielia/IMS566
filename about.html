About UiTM Student e-Profile

The UiTM Student e-Profile refers to a comprehensive digital platform that serves as a central hub for Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) students to manage their academic and non-academic information, access various university services, and stay updated on important announcements. It's essentially a personalized online portal designed to streamline their university experience.

The e-profile typically stores basic student information like their name, student ID, contact details, and program of study. This ensures the university has up-to-date records for communication and administrative purposes.
